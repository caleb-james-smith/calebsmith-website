About

My name is Caleb Smith.

I grew up in Colorado Springs, Colorado. If you have never been there, I recommend visiting! It is a beautiful place. I enjoy hiking at Pikes Peak, the Manitou Incline, Red Rocks, and the Garden of the Gods.

I live in Lawrence, Kansas with my wife, Katie. I work at the University of Kansas as a postdoc in physics. Katie works for various university presses as a freelance editor.
